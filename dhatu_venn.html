lightbulb Key Observations

Mental Object is the great bridge

It belongs to 9 of 10 classifications — every set except Mutually Obstructive. It uniquely bridges the material side (Physical Form, Outer) with the mental side (Formless, Concepts, Focus).

Three identical sets

Formless Realm = Elements with Concepts — both contain exactly {Mind Faculty, Mental Object, Mind Consciousness}. Undefiling adds unconditioned things beyond the 18 dhatus.

Mind Faculty = Mind Consciousness

These two dhatus have identical classification membership across all 10 sets, despite being in different triads (faculty vs. consciousness).

The material / mental divide

The 5 sense faculties and 5 sense objects cluster in Physical Form / Mutually Obstructive. The 6 consciousnesses cluster in Focus. Mind Faculty and Mental Object span both sides.
