## Data sources

- **Feelings → sections:** `texts/full_text_feeling_advice_mapping.json` (`feelingToSections`)
- **Sections → verse refs:** `texts/verse_hierarchy_map.json` (each section has a `verses` array, e.g. `["7.4"]`, `["7.17","7.18","7.19"]`)
- **Verse text:** `texts/root_text.txt` (verses tagged with e.g. `[7.4]`)

To get verses for any section: look up the section `path` in `verse_hierarchy_map.json` and read the `verses` array; then find those references in the root text.
